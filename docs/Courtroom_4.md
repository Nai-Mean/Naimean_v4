# Medication Canon

## Purpose
The purpose of the Medication Canon is to provide a comprehensive, authoritative, and standardized reference for all medications, procedures, and protocols within the Trojan Courthouse. It ensures consistency, safety, and legal compliance in all medical and pharmaceutical operations.

## Intended Audience
- Medical Practitioners
- Pharmacists
- Legal Counsel
- AI Core Administrators

## Medication Canon Structure
The Canon is organized into several key sections:
1. **Core Medications**: Essential drugs used for routine operations.
2. **Specialty Protocols**: Procedures for high-risk or complex cases.
3. **Emergency Responses**: Rapid-action protocols for critical situations.
4. **Legal Framework**: Regulatory and compliance documentation.

## Core Principles
- **Accuracy**: Every entry must be verified by the AI Core.
- **Traceability**: All changes and applications are logged.
- **Safety**: Patient safety is the primary metric for success.
