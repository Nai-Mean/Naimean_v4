# Courtroom Plan Long

## [Vision and Philosophy](Courtroom_2.md)
Odysseus is a multi-agent adjudication system designed to establish Canon through examination rather than authority. Rather than relying upon a single model response, Odysseus convenes a Court where independent witnesses review, findings are challenged, and a consensus is reached through voting and arbitration. Truth creates authority, and reputation follows performance.

## [Court Operations](Courtroom_3.md)
Court Operations transform testimony into Canon through investigation. Proceedings start with a Medication Filing. Witnesses produce independent testimony, followed by Cross Examination where witnesses defend or clarify findings. The Stenographer constructs fact families and voting packets for evaluation. Deadlocks are arbitrated by the Judge.

## [Medication Canon](Courtroom_4.md)
The Medication Canon is the official output for clinical review by Providers, Nurses, and Pharmacists. It prioritizes Patient Safety and Clinical Utility. It includes Canon Confidence Scores based on vote strength, agreement, and safety priority.

## [AI Core Administration](Courtroom_5.md)
The AI Core manages model configuration, witness assignment, and the Trojan Protocol for anonymity. It assigns Court Identities (e.g., HAL-9, JARVIS, ATHENA) to conceal underlying model attribution, ensuring blind adjudication.
