# Trojan Courthouse

## Vision Statement
Odysseus is a multi-agent adjudication system designed to establish Canon through examination rather than authority.

Rather than relying upon a single model response, Odysseus convenes a Court.
Multiple independent witnesses review the same case.
Findings are challenged.
Findings are defended.
Findings are voted upon.
Deadlocks are arbitrated.

Only after examination does a finding become Canon.

The objective is not to produce answers.
The objective is to produce defensible conclusions.

## Core Philosophy
The Trojan Courthouse is founded upon a simple principle:
Truth is established through examination rather than authority.

No witness is presumed correct.
No model is presumed superior.
No vendor receives preferential treatment.

Every finding must survive:
- Testimony
- Cross Examination
- Voting
- Arbitration
- Canon Review

Reputation follows performance.
Authority does not create truth.
Truth creates authority.

## The Courthouse
The Trojan Courthouse serves as the public face of Odysseus. Every major function of the system is represented by a physical location within the courthouse. The courthouse is both a user interface and a conceptual model.

The courthouse exists above the AI Core.
The AI Core exists beneath the courthouse.

### Judge’s Bench
The Judge’s Bench represents arbitration authority.
The Judge does not create findings.
The Judge does not modify findings.
The Judge does not participate in testimony.
The Judge resolves deadlocks.

The Judge’s Stand also serves as the entrance to the AI Core. Authorized users may access administrative functions through this location.

### Witness Stand
The Witness Stand represents independent testimony.
Witnesses review cases in isolation.
Witnesses do not initially see the work of other witnesses.

Each witness provides:
- Findings
- Recommendations
- Challenges
- Supporting rationale

Witnesses participate in:
- Testimony
- Cross Examination
- Voting

Witnesses possess equal authority.
No witness receives weighted voting rights.

### Stenographer Area
The Stenographer serves as a Court Officer.
The Stenographer is not a witness.
The Stenographer remains blinded by the Trojan Protocol.
The Stenographer does not know:
- Witness identities
- Underlying model identities
- Historical performance

Responsibilities include:
- Claim Extraction
- Fact Family Construction
- Duplicate Identification
- Voting Packet Generation
- Court Record Preservation

The Stenographer also participates in voting. The fourth vote exists to permit deadlocks and judicial review.

### Bailiff Area
The Bailiff serves as the custodian of the proceeding.
The Bailiff does not:
- Testify
- Vote
- Participate in Cross Examination
- Participate in Arbitration

The Bailiff becomes active only after adjudication is complete.

Responsibilities include:
- Attribution Restoration
- Performance Tracking
- Canon Publication
- Statistics Updates
- Hall of Canon Archival

The Bailiff announces the ruling.
The Bailiff identifies the top contributors.
The Bailiff updates long-term performance records.

### Hall of Canon
The Hall of Canon is the permanent archive of Odysseus.
Every completed proceeding is preserved.
Nothing is deleted.
Everything survives.

The Hall of Canon contains:
- Testimony
- Cross Examination
- Fact Families
- Voting Records
- Judge Rulings
- Bailiff Reports
- Canon Publications
- Audit Logs

The Hall of Canon serves as the institutional memory of the Court.

### Big Screen
The Big Screen serves as the public display of Court output.

Examples include:
- Active Proceedings
- Canon Publications
- Bailiff Reports
- Statistics
- Model Performance

When the Court is unable to convene due to insufficient case information, direct model responses may be displayed on the Big Screen, clearly marked as "Unverified."

### AI Core Entrance
The Judge’s Stand provides access to the AI Core.
The AI Core exists beneath the courthouse.

The AI Core is responsible for:
- Model Management
- Court Configuration
- API Management
- Statistics
- Authentication
- Hall of Canon Administration

The AI Core administers proceedings.
The AI Core does not influence outcomes.

## Trojan Protocol
The Trojan Protocol preserves anonymity during proceedings. Court identities are assigned independently of underlying models. Participants evaluate testimony. Participants do not evaluate model names. Attribution remains concealed until proceedings conclude.

## Separation of Powers
- **Witnesses**: Testify, Challenge, Vote
- **Stenographer**: Extract, Organize, Vote
- **Judge**: Arbitrate Deadlocks
- **Bailiff**: Publish, Archive, Track Performance
- **AI Core**: Administer, Configure, Monitor

No single role controls the proceeding. Truth emerges from the interaction of independent participants.

## Court Motto
Truth is not generated.
Truth is examined.
Truth is challenged.
Truth is judged.
Truth is verified.

Only then does truth become Canon.
