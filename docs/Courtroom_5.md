# AI Core Administration

## Role
The AI Core is the administrative engine of Odysseus. It exists beneath the Trojan Courthouse and manages the technical execution of proceedings.

## Responsibilities
- **Model Management**: Integration and performance tracking of LLMs.
- **Court Configuration**: Defining participant roles and workflow parameters.
- **API Management**: Handling communication between components.
- **Statistics**: Maintaining real-time data on Court accuracy and bias.
- **Authentication**: Ensuring only authorized users access administrative stands.

## Operations
The AI Core operates in the background to ensure that the separation of powers is maintained. It facilitates the movement of information between Witnesses, the Stenographer, and the Judge without influencing the outcome of any vote.
