Overview

The Trojan Courthouse is the primary adjudication system of the Naimean ecosystem.

Unlike traditional AI systems that rely upon a single model and a single response, the Trojan Courthouse treats every medication review as a formal case.

Cases are filed.

Witnesses testify.

Witnesses challenge one another.

Arguments are examined.

Judgment is rendered.

The Bailiff certifies the proceeding.

The final Canon is displayed.

The complete record is archived.

The objective is not merely to generate answers.

The objective is to establish trustworthy Canon through transparent examination.

Primary Interface:

https://naimean.com/courtroom

Claim-Centric Architecture

Everything in the Trojan Courthouse revolves around Claims.

Not conversations.

Not transcripts.

Not chats.

Claims.

Example:

{
  "claim_id": "C-014",
  "finding": "Monitor for worsening withdrawal symptoms.",
  "votes": 4,
  "status": "accepted",
  "canonical": true
}

Every witness statement becomes one or more claims.

Claims are the atomic unit of the Court.

Principle Of Equal Witnesses

No witness enters proceedings with presumed authority.

No witness enters proceedings with presumed correctness.

Truth is established through:

* Testimony
* Cross Examination
* Judgment
* Verification
* Canon

Reputation follows performance.

The Stenographer

The Stenographer is a preservation and annotation system.

The Stenographer preserves all testimony.

The Stenographer never merges findings.

The Stenographer never deletes findings.

The Stenographer never rewrites findings.

Responsibilities:

* Preserve testimony
* Preserve revisions
* Preserve challenges
* Preserve votes
* Assign Claim IDs
* Extract Claims
* Track Claims
* Hide witness identities
* Annotate findings
* Prepare Judge transcript

Every claim survives.

Nothing is lost.

The Judge

The Judge is an evaluator, not an author.

The Judge does not:

* Create findings
* Modify findings
* Delete findings
* Introduce evidence

The Judge:

* Scores findings
* Ranks findings
* Establishes priority
* Selects Canon candidates
* Selects preferred existing wording
* Resolves ties
* Issues final rulings

The Judge evaluates testimony.

The Judge does not author testimony.

⸻

The Bailiff

The Bailiff is the final guardian of the proceeding.

Responsibilities:

* Verify procedural integrity
* Maintain witness assignment records
* Track performance statistics
* Display final Canon
* Archive proceedings
* Escort cases into the Hall of Canon

The Bailiff does not:

* Vote
* Testify
* Judge

The structure is intentionally ordered by clinical usefulness within inpatient behavioral health and addiction medicine environments.
